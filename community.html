Community Programs for Immigrants in North Carolina District 4

North Carolina Association for Immigrants (NCA4I)

Location: Statewide, NC

Services: NCA4I offers online ESL classes, newcomer orientation sessions, and a resource center to assist immigrants in adjusting to life in North Carolina. They also provide training for community organizations and public agencies serving immigrants.

Visit NCA4I Website

Refugee Support Center

Location: Chapel Hill, NC

Services: The Refugee Support Center offers employment services, financial literacy programs, housing assistance, and immigration services to support refugees in rebuilding their lives in the U.S.

Visit Refugee Support Center

El Centro Hispano

Location: Durham, NC

Services: Provides education, support, and healthcare services to the Latino community. They offer ESL classes, legal clinics, and other resources to help immigrants integrate into the community.

Learn More

Siembra NC

Location: Statewide, NC

Services: Siembra NC is a grassroots immigrant rights group that advocates for immigrant protections and offers support through initiatives like the "Fourth Amendment Workplace" campaign, aiming to protect workers from unlawful detainment.

Learn More

Church World Service (CWS) Greensboro

Location: Greensboro, NC

Services: CWS Greensboro offers the NC-Immigrant Solidarity Fund, providing one-time cash assistance grants to vulnerable immigrant families for housing, utility bills, and other emergency financial needs.

Visit CWS Greensboro

Raleigh Immigrant Community (RIC)

Location: Raleigh, NC

Services: RIC focuses on integration and empowerment programs, offering transition support, skill development workshops, and cultural heritage initiatives to foster inclusivity for refugees and immigrants.

Visit RIC Website
