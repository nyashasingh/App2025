Immigration Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a visa?

A visa is an official authorization that allows a foreign national to enter, stay, and sometimes work or study in a country for a specified period. In the United States, there are different types of visas, including:

  • Tourist visa (B-2) – for short visits for tourism or business purposes.
  • Student visa (F-1) – for individuals who wish to study in the U.S.
  • Work visa – for foreign nationals seeking to work in the U.S. (e.g., H-1B for specialty workers).
  • Immigrant visa – for individuals seeking permanent residency in the U.S. (Green Card).

Each visa type has specific requirements and conditions that must be met before being granted entry into the U.S.
