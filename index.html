Welcome to Immigration Helper!

This app helps immigrants find legal resources, check visa options, and get support for their immigration journey.

What Can You Find Here?

  • Check your visa eligibility
  • Find an immigration lawyer
  • Learn about common immigration terms
  • Access helpful USCIS links

How to Navigate:

  • Use the tabs at the top to explore different parts of the app
  • If you need to switch your language, click the top-right corner
  • Visit the FAQ page

Lawyer Directory

Search for qualified immigration lawyers based on specialization, language, cost, and location.

  • Filters: Language, Pro bono / Low-cost, Location-based.
  • Lawyer Profiles - View profiles with names, photos, specializations, and contact info.

Immigration FAQ

Access templates for various immigration forms and FAQs to guide you through the process.

  • FAQ Section - Common questions about immigration forms and processes (e.g., “What do I need to apply for asylum?”).

Educational Resources

Learn about your rights and stay updated with immigration news.

  • Know Your Rights - Information on your rights in different situations (e.g., what to do if ICE knocks on your door).
  • Immigration News - Important updates about changes in immigration laws and policies.

Community Programs

Access local immigrant support programs like English classes, citizenship classes, and workforce development programs.

  • English Language Classes
  • Citizenship Classes
  • Workforce Development Programs
